About the site:

The recipes listed here are ones that I have developed myself. In some cases, I started with a recipe found in a cookbook or magazine, but over time, I've adapted it enough to call it my own.

The site also includes links to my favorite online cooking resources. One day, I hope to include a section of restaurant reviews.
