About Us

Here at Greenwave, we aim to develop ceramic works of all kind, from all types of people.

From beginners to experts, we offer classes and FREE studio hours to everyone.

What started as a small, after-school club in an open garage, turned into a widedpread community.

Greenwave focuses on teaching not necessarily on how perfect a work is, but rather the technique used to get there.

We strive for works from the heart, to the hands.
